BTB

Built To Ball

A breakdown of the NBA's genetic history

Basketball is a game of endurance, precision, agility, and speed. It takes years of dedication and hard work to become a professional athlete. For basketball however, it can take a little more than just that. Out of the four major professional sports leagues, the NBA is the only league where the players can be spotted at any public venue, simply because of their height.

This site is dedicated to exploring the significance of a player's genetic makeup in their ability to make it to the NBA and how it has impacted the game of basketball over the years.
